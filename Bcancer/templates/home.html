What is Breast Cancer?

Breast cancer is a type of cancer that begins in the cells of the breast. It is one of the most common cancers among women worldwide. While men can also get breast cancer, it is far less common. Early detection is crucial for improving survival rates and outcomes.

Regular screening and self-exams help in detecting abnormal changes in the breast tissue, which may indicate the presence of cancer.

Why Awareness Matters

Breast cancer is one of the most common cancers affecting women. Early detection and education play a crucial role in treatment and survival rates.
